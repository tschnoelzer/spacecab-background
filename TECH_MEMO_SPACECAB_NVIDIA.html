Critical Integration Requirements

1. Thermal Interface: Nano-coating applied directly to Orin heat spreader. Thermal pads standardized. Target: <40¬∞C junction temp at 50¬∞C ambient.

2. Power Stability: HYVEN battery voltage regulation must maintain ¬±5% tolerance for Orin stability. Transient response <10ms.

3. Vibration Isolation: Orin module mounted on dampened chassis. Road vibration isolation required for sensor alignment.

4. EMC Shielding: Integrated Faraday cage around compute module. Prevents interference with 77GHz radar.
