## PHASE 2: ECONOMIC WARFARE (Months 4-12)
### Objective: Make competition economically unviable

### 2.1 Predatory Pricing (Controlled)
**Action:** Bleed competitors dry

- **Launch Promo:** First ride free, next 5 rides 50% off (subsidized by HYVEN)
- **Price Matching:** Always 10% cheaper than Careem/Uber for comparable routes
- **Subscription Model:** AED 299/month unlimited rides (destroys per-ride economics)
- **Corporate Rates:** 30% off for companies with 50+ employees (B2B lock-in)

**Killer Move:** Competitors can't match our cost structure (no driver, solar charging). We can lose money longer than they can survive.

### 2.2 Driver Poaching Campaign
**Action:** Strip competitors of their workforce

- **Taxi Driver Transition Program:** Retrain 500 Dubai Taxi drivers as SpaceCab fleet monitors
- **Higher Wages:** 20% above RTA rates for operations roles
- **Future-Proofing:** Market as "upgrade your career to autonomous fleet management"

**Killer Move:** Create driver shortage for traditional taxis. Service quality drops; we look better by comparison.

### 2.3 Data Domination
**Action:** The network effect that compounds

- **Route Optimization:** Every ride improves algorithm; competitors start from zero
- **Predictive Deployment:** AI places vehicles before demand spikes (concerts, flights, rain)
- **Customer Behavior:** 6 months of ride data = unbeatable personalization

**Killer Move:** By Month 12, we know Dubai's mobility patterns better than RTA. That's a moat.
